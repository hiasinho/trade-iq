Privacy Policy

Thanks for using trade-iq.com or visiting one of our websites. This policy explains the what, how, and why of the information we collect when you visit one of our websites, or when you use our Services. It also explains the specific ways we use and disclose that information. We take your privacy extremely seriously, and we never sell lists or email addresses.

Website Visitors

From time to time, trade-iq.com may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

trade-iq.com also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. trade-iq.com does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to trade-iq.com choose to interact with trade-iq.com in ways that require trade-iq.com to gather personally-identifying information. Aggregated Statistics

trade-iq.com may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. However, trade-iq.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. trade-iq.com may use cookies to help trade-iq.com identify and track visitors, their usage of trade-iq.com, and their website access preferences. trade-iq.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using trade-iq.com’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of trade-iq.com’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
